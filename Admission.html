Registration

The Admission forms are obtainable at the school's Admission Office. Candidate must have they former school certificate

Requirements

After The Admission forms has been obtained
it should be retured to the school with the following:

  • 2 candidate and 4 parent passport sized photographs;
  • 2 Photocopy of birth certificate;
  • 1 Photocopy of BECE (Basic Education Certificate Examination)
    or WAEC (West African Examination Certificate) result of their former school
  • LASSRA (Lagos State Residents' Registration Agency)
  • Tax Clearance

    • Procedures

  • Candidates that has cleared their registraton at the admission office
    • are to sit for the entrance examination coming up in the college
  • The examination will hold three weeks after the Registration
    All Candidates are to submit before the examination no candidate
    are permmited during the examination
  • Candidates must have at least 75% percentage score of the exam
    and have a satisfactory BECE (Basic Education Certificate Examination)
    and WAEC (West African Examination Certificate)
