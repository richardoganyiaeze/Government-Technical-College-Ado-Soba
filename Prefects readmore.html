INTRODUCING THE NEW STUDENT LEDADERS

We are excited to announced the decoration the Student Representative Council for the 2024/2025 Academic Session of Government Technical College Ado Soba. This council will serve as a platform for student leadership and coordination, representing all the prefects, governors and assistant governors within the college.

Congratulations to Happiness Ejewi (Motor Vehicle Mechanics Works), Our new HEAD BOY, and Amu Gift(Catering), our HEAD GIRL. These outstanding leaders will guide and inspire their peers towards excellence. We'are proud to see this new generation of leaders rise and take charge!

Let's celebrate their commitment to leadership, growth, and teamwork!
