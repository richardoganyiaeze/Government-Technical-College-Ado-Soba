Construction

Plumbing and pipefitting

is a specialized field that deals with knowledge and hand-on skills need to install, maintain, and repair plumbing systems. Students will learn about pipefitting fixtures, water supply and drainage systems, as well as heating and ventilation systems. The course covers essential skills such as pipe fitting, soldering, welding, system troubleshooting, and understanding plumbing codes and regulations. Emphasis is placed on safety standards and efficient problem-solving techniques. Graduates of the course are prepared to work in residenial, commercial, and industrial plumbing, with a strong foundation in both repair and installation tasks
