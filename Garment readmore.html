Garment Making

is a specialized field that deals with design, cut, sew, and finish custom clothing. Stsudents will learn about fabric selection, pattern making, garment construction techniques, and the use of various sewing tools and machines. The section covers key topics such as fitting, stitching, hemming, and adding finishing touches like buttons, zippers, and trims, Emphasis is placed on both creativity and technical precision, allowing students to create high-quality functional garments. Whether for fashion design or custom tailoring, this section prepares students for careers in the fashion industry, clothing production, or starting their own garment-making business.
