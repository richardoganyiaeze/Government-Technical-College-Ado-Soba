Engineering

Motor Vehicle Mechanics

is a specialized field that deals with the repair, maintenance, and servicing of cars, trucks, and other motorized vehicles. Students learn about the operation of cars, trucks, and other motorized vehicles. Students learn about the operation of internal combustion engines, electrical systems, transmission, suspension, braking systems, diagonistics, and vehicle troubleshooting. The section also covers modern technologies like hybrid and electric vehicles, ensuring students are equipped to work with range of vehicle types. Graduates gain the expertise needed to work in automotive repair shops, dealership, or pursue careers in vehicle maintenance and design.
